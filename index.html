About phaserbsc.com

An original product offering more sustainable and accessible passive income opportunities.

phaserbsc hosts a network of yield-generating dApps which will be launched in a series of phases. Phase I will involve a contract presale offering discounted PHX tokens to investors, and an auction acting as an ICO simulator which will immediately generate dividends for PHX-BNB LP token stakers. Phase II will be launched at the end of the ICO and will involve the deployment of more innovative yield generating dApps.

FAQS

Frequently asked questions

  • What is phaserbsc.com?

    phaserbsc is Phase I of the wider Phaser Protocol, which hosts a year-long yield-generating endogenous ICO. Simply put, there will be a daily auction in which a decreasing amount of PHX tokens are sold. The BNB generated from those auctions will then be directly distrIbuted to stakers of PHX-BNB LPs.

    What does the public presale offer?

    The public presale offers a limit amount of tokens at a huge discount which grants early believers in the potential of our protocol the ability to realize considerable profits - Our presale represents a chance to get in early on something unique and great.

    Is the public presale safe?

    Yes. The public presale will be a programmable presale conducted through a smart contract coded in solidity using tried-and-tested best practices. Investors will receive PHX tokens immediately upon sending BNB to the presale contract. The contract will be verified on BSCSCAN.
